Services & Offerings

Comprehensive Septic & Drain Services solutions for Antelope and the surrounding Sacramento County area

We've been handling septic and drain issues in Antelope since 2003, and honestly, it's pretty straightforward work once you know what you're doing. Most of our calls are routine septic pumping — that's the preventative maintenance that keeps your system running smoothly. But we also deal with drain problems, backups, and system repairs when things go wrong. If you're out here in Antelope or the surrounding area without city sewer, you're relying on your septic system, and we make sure it stays healthy.
